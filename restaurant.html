Chef’s Special

Our Chef’s Special features two signature dishes carefully curated by our head chef, highlighting both vegetarian and non-vegetarian excellence.

Vegetarian Special: Paneer Tikka Masala – Soft cottage cheese cubes marinated in aromatic spices and cooked in a rich, creamy tomato-based gravy.

Non-Vegetarian Special: Butter Chicken – Tender chicken pieces simmered in a smooth, buttery sauce infused with traditional Indian spices.

These dishes represent our commitment to authentic taste, premium ingredients, and exceptional culinary standards.
